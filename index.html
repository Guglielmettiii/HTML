About me

Ciao! I am Sara Guglielmetti, a UX UI Designer with a passion for creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences. I started my studies in Spain, where I attended my first UX/UI Design school. This experience abroad allowed me to immerse myself in a creative and international environment, which strongly influenced my approach to design.

When I'm not working, I love traveling around Italy with my partner and our faithful dog. Together, we immerse ourselves in the local culture, tasting the typical dishes of the various regions. Our passion for food leads us to explore hidden trattorias and restaurants, always looking for new flavors and culinary traditions to discover.

Besides traveling and cooking, I am a huge fan of anime and horror movies. I love losing myself in the gripping stories of anime and feeling the adrenaline rush of horror movies, which often inspire my creativity in design as well.
